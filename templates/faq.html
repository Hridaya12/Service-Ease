Frequently Asked Questions

ServiceEase is an online platform that connects users with skilled and reliable electricians and plumbers for various home service needs.

Users can browse through our platform, submit service requests, and choose from a list of qualified electricians and plumbers. Service providers will then contact the user directly to discuss the details and schedule the service.

We are continuously expanding our services. Please check our website or contact our support team to confirm if we currently operate in your location.

Simply create an account, submit a service request specifying your needs, and choose from the available service providers. Once you make a selection, the chosen professional will contact you to finalize the details.
